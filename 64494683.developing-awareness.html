The Main Message

If you would understand strategic thinking for the 21st century, there is a small but critical body of work that you need to understand. If you don’t understand this critical body of work, all of the conversations that are happening in the boardroom are going to involve concepts, metaphors and frameworks that will go right over your head and destroy your pitch before you even get started.

The following videos and lectures will give you the proper mental models and verbiage to understand just what the hell people are talking about during meetings. When you understand what they’re saying, you’ll understand that they’re full of shit.

When you understand that they’re full of shit, you can employ the appropriate language to submit your point of view in a language that they’ll get.

Remember: what the learner learns is a function of what they already know (Kullback-Leibler Divergence).
