The Two Types Of Sales

There are two types of sales:

  • Simple Sales, where the sale takes one call.

  • Complex Sales, where the sale takes more than one call and relies on multiple decision-makers.

When your sale depends on multiple people agreeing with each other, you’re in a complex sale…and complex sales are complex because people are complex.

People don’t make up their mind. It would be so easy if people could make up their mind! But they don’t make up their mind. Their minds are always changing. They can go from yes to no to yes to no, all in the same meeting.
