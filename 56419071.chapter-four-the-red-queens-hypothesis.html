Alice is about to receive a crash course in Evolution and Creativity. In fact, the Red Queen is the embodiment of Evolutionary Creativity itself.

Alice, having failed to get along with the Red Queen in her first encounter, is still lucky enough to follow the Red Queen to the top of a little hill overlooking the country.

Janusian Progression

The Red Queen Hypothesis states that,

"...organisms must constantly adapt, evolve, and proliferate not merely to gain reproductive advantage, but also simply to survive while pitted against ever-evolving opposing organisms in a constantly changing environment."

Even though the Red Queen seems to be a harsh Professor, she is trying to show Alice how competition works in the real world.
